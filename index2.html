Good location located in the centre of Lenton amongst all other student housing. Large bedrooms with two fully functioning bathrooms which we have had no problems with all year. Dishwasher in the kitchen with a washing machine and tumble dryer that have worked well all year. Henry T - recommends this property.
Great property, refurbished and modernised in the Summer 2014. We have enjoyed the property so much that we have stayed in it for 2 consecutive years whereas most people change houses after a year- by doing so, the landlord agrees to maintain rent price for the following year at the original price. The house is great but unfortunately it is a bit further from the University- we never had a problem with the distance in our second year but for 3rd year, I would probably recommend going a bit closer to uni purely for attendance purposes! All a matter of preference though. It takes me about 15-20 minutes from door to lecture hall. All the rooms are good size, furniture all in tact and new. Parking is easy enough and you can usually find a space just outside the property or nearby. Any problems usually get resolved and fixed quickly too which is ideal! Aldi and tesco express are at the end of the road, en route back from uni which is highly convenient. There is a garden but it is never used. Overall, I would definitely recommend this property.
