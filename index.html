ABOUT US

Hello! Welcome to StudentBricks! Our ultimate aim is to make the experience of student housing as easy and as stress free as possible.
This idea first came to us when we found ourselves in the unique position of starting university whilst simultaneously working in the student lettings market in Birmingham. We quickly realised that for a rental market that boasts huge growth in the UK, the experience and reputation it was receiving was well below par!

We decided to create this website to cover three factors:

Improve the Student Experience
Boost Market Efficiency + Modernise Student Rental Market
Bring accountability to landlords & estate agents

We feel this is the first step to the eradication of those terrible student housing stories you hear and the estate agents & landlords that thrive off them. We will be working hard to guide you to the best landlords & agents (yes, they do exist!) meaning you have more time to enjoy university rather than stressing about quality of the roof above your head.
The website allows you to navigate through reviews from previous tenants, write about your own experiences and then select and view the property that catches your eye! Saving you time and hassle.
We have many new features we are looking to roll out over a different number of phases as the website grows! If you would like to get involved or would like to contact us please feel free to email us at: info@studentbricks.co.uk
Thanks for taking the time to visit our website and we hope it helps you find that perfect student house!
